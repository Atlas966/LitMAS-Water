# LitMAS Water 3

## Advanced Unity water shader and system built for BONELAB, created in Amplify
<img width="500" height="500" alt="LW3 Icon" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f8055900-0fa3-4aa5-9558-10eea3575bd2" />

## Installation Guide
Installing LitMAS Water is simple:
Scroll down to **releases**

<img width="500" height="854" alt="ReleaseButton" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d9237226-7918-4482-b3dc-576ba93e0aa0" />

Download the newest available release

<img width="500" height="825" alt="DownloadButton" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8938db56-40a5-4542-8177-eff7566f94cb" />

Open it, and import to your project. LitMAS Water installs to `Assets/LitMAS Water`. Open the `/Demo Scene` folder and take a peek at the demo scene to get started.

# Feature Set
## Depth Masked Refaction
  - Mask out ugly ring around objects infront of the water surface with the flip of a switch
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="DMR" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4333299c-df9a-402e-a0c8-40d01b7b6406" />

## Caustics
  - Project caustics onto any object inside the water volume
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="Caustics" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3ed906fa-b877-4e59-baa1-7ec1b0e29e73" />

## Anti-Tiling
  - Most surface features have an optional anti-tiling toggle, allowing them to appear varried and not tiled at any distance
## Micro Normals
  - Extremely small normals blended with regular normals, giving the water surface an extremely detailed presentation when viewed up close
  - Micro Normals have a controlable fading distnace, so they seamlessly fade away when the camera is far enough from the water surface
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="MicroNormal" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/162ff918-c079-440f-9ea5-7b13a463ab45" />

## Rain Drop Ripples
  - Simulate rain drop ripples on the water surface
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="RainDropRipples" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/221077c1-a6f1-4260-90db-8d61f83dfd36" 
  />
## Flowmapping
  - Custom built flowmap authoring tool comes with the package, allowing you to guide the flow of the water
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="UV Flowmapping" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/3cf38138-fa68-4d98-b8b3-81214f412268" />

## Distorted UV's
  - An optional feature, UV distortion can give the water surface a unique appearance
  - Using the included Wavey Flowmap can give the water the appearace of waves moving across the surface
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="UV Distorted" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b41f4d02-abe3-46b9-9b9b-3fc87f6d3430" />

## Waves
  - 5 different type of wave modes to fine tune visuals
    - Gerstner Waves
    - 3D Texture Waves
    - Gerster + 3D Texture Waves
    - Procedural Noise Waves
    - Dynamic Ripple Waves
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="Waves" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f00a6e2d-3e64-41f4-8cd7-8150fe12bf00" />

## Foam
  - Various setting to tweak foam visuals
  - Parallax
  - Optional distortion for unique visuals
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="Foam" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b412171c-d882-4a01-8157-8009e6bdfef2" />

## Dynamic Ripples
  - Dynamic ripples via custom logic, particles, custom shader, and camera
  - Pre-placed developer ripples for maps; Simply drag in a Ripple Particle Emitter into your water and adjust settings
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="Dynamic Ripples" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e720e2d0-fe42-473a-9873-2bd2513c5220" />

## Depth Colors
  - Adds fog to water based on depth, lerped between 2 different colors
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="DepthColors" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/55a72985-c83c-4f37-ba7d-78f942f7438c" />

## Post Processing 
  - Handful of post processing options to fine tune and stylize the visual appearance of the water color
<img width="1000" height="1118" alt="PP" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2e99cec4-f89a-4364-a406-9c13e5fb843e" />

## Alpha Masking
  - Custom alpha mask authoring tool comes with the package, allowing you to cut out holes in the water surface
